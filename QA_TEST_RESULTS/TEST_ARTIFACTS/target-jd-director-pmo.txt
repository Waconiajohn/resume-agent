THE ROLE
We are seeking an experienced Director of PMO to establish and lead our enterprise Project Management Office. This is a high-visibility leadership role responsible for standardizing project delivery, driving portfolio governance, and ensuring our $25M+ annual project portfolio delivers measurable business outcomes.

You will build and lead a team of 12-15 project managers, establish PMO governance from the ground up, and serve as a trusted advisor to the C-suite on project investment decisions.

- Develop and implement standardized project delivery methodologies (Agile, SAFe, hybrid) appropriate to each business unit

- Develop project management career paths and competency frameworks for the PM team
- Ensure compliance with SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and relevant regulatory requirements for project deliverables
- Champion continuous improvement through retrospectives, lessons learned, and PMO maturity assessments
