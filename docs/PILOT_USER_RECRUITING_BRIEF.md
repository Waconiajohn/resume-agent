# Pilot User Recruiting Brief

## Objective

Recruit the first 5 participants for observed launch-readiness sessions.

We are not looking for generic feedback. We are looking for people who can realistically try to:

1. build profile context
2. tailor a resume
3. find and shortlist jobs
4. try Smart Referrals

## Who To Recruit

Prioritize people who are:

- actively searching now
- expecting to search within 90 days
- updating their resume or LinkedIn soon
- willing to think out loud while using a live product

### Best participant profiles

1. senior leader
2. mid-career manager
3. industry switcher
4. LinkedIn-heavy searcher
5. job-board-first searcher

## Who Not To Recruit For Round 1

- internal builders who already know the product too well
- people who are not job-searching and have no near-term intent
- people who need a fully guided onboarding call just to begin

## Suggested Incentive

Pick one and keep it simple:

1. gift card
2. free month / discounted access
3. free premium review session later

## Outreach Message

Use something this short:

> I’m running a small set of 30 to 45 minute product sessions with people who are actively job searching or preparing to search soon. You’d be using the product for realistic resume, job search, and referral tasks while thinking out loud. If you’re open to it, I’d love to schedule one this week.

## Intake Questions

Ask only what helps with segmentation:

1. Are you actively job searching right now?
2. If not, do you expect to start within the next 90 days?
3. Are you mostly using LinkedIn, job boards, referrals, or a mix?
4. Are you pursuing the same kind of role or changing direction?
5. Have you used resume or job-search tools recently?

## Scheduling Guidance

Keep sessions:

- 30 to 45 minutes
- one participant per slot
- no more than 3 in one day if the same person is moderating and synthesizing notes

## Recruitment Target Mix

| Participant | Target profile | Search style |
|---|---|---|
| P1 | Senior leader | referrals + selective roles |
| P2 | Mid-career manager | job boards + pipeline |
| P3 | Industry switcher | benchmark/story heavy |
| P4 | LinkedIn-focused user | profile + reputation |
| P5 | Referral-focused user | network + outreach |

## Success Criteria

Recruiting is good enough for round 1 if:

1. at least 5 participants are scheduled
2. at least 2 are fresh to the product
3. at least 1 is referral-driven
4. at least 1 is heavily job-board-driven
