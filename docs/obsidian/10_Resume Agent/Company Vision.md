# CareerIQ Company Vision

> Source: Google Drive `Agentic.AI Company/` — 30 planning documents synthesized 2026-03-06

## Brands

- **CareerIQ.app** — B2C consumer platform
- **FirstSourceTeam.com** — B2B corporate outplacement

## Mission

Restore the American Retirement Dream by combining AI-powered career positioning with integrated retirement planning for displaced executives 45+.

## Vision

Be the lifelong branding partner for every professional in transition, combining decades of coaching expertise with agentic AI.

## Three Mission Pillars

1. **Extract the Real Story** — surface the other 99% of career value
2. **Create the Perfect Match** — career matchmaking, not resume optimization
3. **Secure the Financial Future** — integrated retirement planning

## Win-Win-Win Philosophy (from LifeUSA)

Every transaction must produce winners for:
- The **executive** (career positioned, financial plan, confidence restored)
- The **employer/partner** (better outcomes, lower cost, measurable ROI)
- The **platform** (sustainable revenue, compounding data advantage)

Fourth winner: financial planning partner receives warm, pre-qualified referrals.

## Core Values

| Value | Platform Implication |
|-------|---------------------|
| Best in Class or We Do Not Do It | Every agent must be best-in-market, not generic |
| Everyone Gets Help | Free tier is the business model, not charity |

## Target Market

### Primary ICP

Displaced mid-level executive, age 55 (typical), 20-30 years experience, $120K-$250K+ previous compensation, 1-9 months unemployed.

### Top Functions (highest displacement volume)

1. Product/Process/Project Managers (PMOs cut first as "overhead")
2. IT Executives (cloud migration, outsourcing, AI narratives)
3. Sales Executives (territory consolidation, PE leadership replacement)
4. Operations Executives (M&A redundancies, PE efficiency mandates)
5. Construction Executives (cyclical + structural displacement)

### Four Financial Segments

| Segment | Savings | Platform Priority |
|---------|---------|-------------------|
| 1: Crisis Mode | <$250K | Speed: resume, job matching, interviews |
| 2: Stressed but Stable | $250K-$500K | Full platform engagement |
| 3: Ideal Client | $750K-$1.5M | Complete engagement + retirement planning from week 1 |
| 4: Financially Comfortable | $1.5M+ | Strategic positioning + alternative paths + retirement optimization |

### Universal Psychographic Truths

- They are **invisible** (50-200+ applications, zero responses)
- They are **proud** and resistant to help
- They are **terrified about money** but will not say so
- They are **skeptical of AI** (tried ChatGPT, got generic junk)
- They need to **feel seen** (the deepest retention driver)

### First Five Minutes Rule

User asks three questions internally: (1) Does this understand my situation? (2) Is this worth my time? (3) Can I afford it? All three answered within 5 minutes or they leave.

## Competitive Position

- X-axis: AI Automation Level (Low to High)

3. Integrated retirement planning (financial planner network)
